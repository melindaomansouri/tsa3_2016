There are quite a few names for unmanned aerial vehicles, which generally refer to the same concept. The term drone is more widely used by the public. It was pinned in reference to the resemblance of dumb-looking navigation and loud-and-regular motor sounds of old military unmanned aircraft to the male bee. Unmanned aircraft system emphasizes the importance of other elements beyond an aircraft itself. UAV is defined as a powered, flying vehicle that does not carry a person inside it. It can fly automatically or be piloted remotely and can also be expendable or recoverable.
